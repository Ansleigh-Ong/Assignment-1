About Us

Starting Out

Our company started off with just a pot of plants and our owners unwavering determination to enter the flower business. Our owner started his business selling out of his own garden where he invested all of his time and effort into nuturing the good quality plants you see today!

Building Up

From the hard work put in, Cloverene finally gained enough traction to open a small shop serving several customers. It wasn't that popular but slowly but surely, more people came. Our owner spent even more time and money ensuring the shop's operations are effcient and good-quality for customers.

Present times

Now, Cloverene is all the hype! We have just reach global status and we are only just beginning! You can see our products across global supermarkets, ensuring premium quality for you and your loved ones! Come join us and bloom smiles on your loved ones faces!
